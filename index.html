Company Introduction

Diamond Crusher, operated by Titan Crushing Equipment LLP, is a leading Indian manufacturer of crushing and screening equipment used in construction, mining, infrastructure, and material processing industries. With over 60 years of experience, the company delivers reliable, high-performance machinery that converts large rocks into usable construction materials like sand, gravel, and crushed stone.

What the Company Does

    Machine Design & Engineering:

    creating machines using technical knowledge and engineering methods to ensure high performance, efficiency, and durability.

    Manufacturing & Assembly:

    Producing machine parts and assembling them together to build complete equipment ready for operation.

    Installation & Commissioning:

    Setting up machines at the customer’s site and testing them to make sure they work properly and safely before use.

    Maintenance & After-Sales Support:

    Providing regular servicing, repairs, spare parts, and technical assistance to keep machines running smoothly after purchase.

    Turnkey Crushing Plant Solutions:

    Delivering complete crushing plants from design to final operation, where the company handles everything and provides a ready-to-use system.

Key Products & Equipment

Jaw Crushers

Primary crushing machines used to break large rocks into smaller pieces for mining and construction industries.

Cone Crushers

Secondary and tertiary crushers producing fine aggregates for asphalt and concrete production.

Mobile Crushing Plants

Portable crushing systems designed for flexible operations and quick installation.

Engineering & Manufacturing

Advanced fabrication facilities

Metal cutting using CNC or laser machines, Welding and joining heavy steel structures, Machine shaping and bending of metal plates, Precision machining of crusher components, Assembly of industrial equipment, Quality inspection and testing

Precision machining processes

It is the process of shaping metal components using specialized machines so that every part fits perfectly and works smoothly without errors.

Stringent quality control

Raw materials are inspected before production, Parts are checked during manufacturing, Machines are tested after assembly, Performance and safety tests are conducted, Final inspection before delivery
